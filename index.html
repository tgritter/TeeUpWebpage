WELCOME TO TEE UP!

A golfing app that let's users pair up and compete on leaderboards

Our Services

Match Making

We let users create and join golf games in their local area. Users can also choose to be paired automatically.

Scorecard

We offer a scorecard feature for users to keep track of their rounds. Includes advanced stats tracking.

Game Modes

We offer several game modes including Stroke Play, Match Play, with and without handicap. More games can be access by purchasing the Premieum Model.

Global Leaderboard

Our App is unique in that it uses the Offical Course and Slope Ratings of Golf Courses to calculate a Global Score. This allows users to compete against each other no matter which course they are on.
